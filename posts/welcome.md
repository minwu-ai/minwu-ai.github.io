This is the home for my writing on AI — model risk, governance, evaluation, and the practical questions that come up when these systems move from demos into real organizations.

Most posts here are drafted with an automated pipeline and reviewed before they go live. The goal is consistency: clear, useful analysis published regularly rather than occasionally.

If you work in this space — validation, risk, applied AI — I hope some of it is useful. You can subscribe via [RSS](/feed.xml) to follow along.
